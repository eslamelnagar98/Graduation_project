  • About Us

Cardiovascular diseases (CVDs) are the number 1 cause of death in the world. Health Attack Detection allows us to keep track of heart attack symptoms, which if reported within time and taken care of can save lives. Diagnosing a heart attack requires doctors of reviewing your medical history which can be made easier and more accessible using our system, the doctor will also perform a physical exam and conduct an electrocardiogram (ECG) so that the doctor can monitor your heart's electrical activity and connect with patient. this patient can monitor his heart's electrical activity too. Our system aims to help doctors in the process of diagnosing and detecting heart attacks and Rapid alert for a defect in the electrical activity of the heart.
